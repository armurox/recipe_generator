Instructions
1
Cook penne pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.
2
Season chicken breast with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Slice into strips.
3

4

5
Toss in the cooked pasta and sliced chicken. Add reserved pasta water as needed to loosen the sauce. Serve immediately with grated parmesan.
